Discover.

Before I start building anything, I get curious. I ask questions. I listen. This phase is all about uncovering the why behind a project—what problem are we solving, who needs it solved, and what constraints or opportunities already exist.

I talk with users and stakeholders, dig into research, and map out what success actually looks like. Whether it’s user interviews, competitor audits, or just good old-fashioned note-taking, this is the groundwork that helps the rest of the project run smoothly—and meaningfully.

Design.

Design is where things start to take shape. I translate ideas and insights into wireframes, interfaces, and interaction flows that feel intuitive and human-centered.

This part of the process is hands-on and collaborative. I pay close attention to visual hierarchy, accessibility, and how each element supports the user’s journey. The goal? Clear, beautiful, usable design that reflects your brand and makes people feel at home.

Develop.

Great designs deserve pixel perfect implementation.

That means responsive and compatible. Once the design is in place, I build it out with clean, responsive HTML and CSS that works well on all devices. I keep performance and accessibility in mind, and test along the way to make sure everything holds up across screen sizes.

I'm currently deepening my front-end skills and exploring JavaScript and Python to bring even more interactivity and functionality to future projects.
